Our Purpose

At Summit Rafting Co., we're dedicated to providing unforgettable white water rafting experiences that connect people with nature's raw power and beauty. Our expert guides ensure safe, thrilling adventures for all skill levels.

Our Offerings

From gentle family floats to extreme Class V rapids, we offer a range of trips to suit every adventurer. All equipment is provided, including wetsuits, helmets, and life jackets. No experience necessary for most trips!

Summit Rafting Co. has been providing world-class white water experiences since 2005. Our founder, Bryan Nunez, started with a single raft and a passion for river adventures. Today, we're proud to be the premier rafting company in the region, with thousands of satisfied customers and a perfect safety record.
